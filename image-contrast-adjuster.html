This tool is designed for one image at a time, but don’t worry—it’s super fast, so you can easily do multiple images if needed.

What happens if I overdo the contrast?

Overdoing it can make the light areas too bright or the dark areas too deep, which might lose some of the finer details in your image. Keep it moderate for the best results.
