FAQs

  • Can I set different durations for each task? Yes! Some versions of the tool let you assign custom durations per task. If not, you can at least set a default time that applies to all of them.
  • Does it work if I only have a few hours free? Absolutely. Just enter your start and end time and it’ll squeeze your tasks into that window.
  • What if I don’t finish everything on the list? That’s fine. It’s a schedule, not a contract. Use it as a guide—whatever doesn’t get done can roll over to tomorrow’s random adventure.
  • Can I include breaks or free time blocks? Yep! You can either let the tool auto-insert breaks, or manually add “break” as one of your tasks. Stretch, snack, scroll TikTok… it all counts.
  • Is it okay to reshuffle if I don’t like the order? Totally. Just hit that button again and get a whole new schedule. No judgment—sometimes the vibe’s just off.
