What Is a Rental Yield Calculator?

A Rental Yield Calculator is a tool that helps you figure out how much income you can make from renting out a property, in terms of a percentage return on your investment. It takes into account both the property’s rental income and its current market value (or purchase price) to calculate how much money you're making from it.

Basically, it helps you understand whether your investment property is a good deal or if you're better off looking elsewhere. If you’ve been thinking about buying property to rent out, this calculator is a must-have to quickly assess your potential profit.

FAQs About the Rental Yield Calculator

What is rental yield?

Rental yield is the percentage of the property’s value that you earn from renting it out. It’s calculated by dividing the annual rental income by the property’s value and multiplying by 100.

How do I calculate rental income?

Your annual rental income is simply the amount of rent you earn in one year. If you rent the property monthly, multiply the monthly rent by 12 to get the total yearly income.

Can I use the Rental Yield Calculator for different types of properties?

Yes! Whether you're calculating rental yield for a house, apartment, or commercial property, the formula works the same way for all types of real estate.

Does the Rental Yield Calculator consider expenses?

No, the basic calculator doesn't factor in expenses like maintenance costs, property taxes, or insurance. However, it gives you a good starting point for evaluating a property’s potential.
