FAQs

  1. What makes a password “strong”?
    A strong password is long (at least 12 characters), random, and includes a mix of letters (both uppercase and lowercase), numbers, and symbols. Avoid using obvious patterns like “123456” or your pet’s name.
  2. Will this tool tell me if my password is unbreakable?
    While no password is 100% unbreakable, this tool helps you see if your password is strong enough to resist most common attack methods. It gives you tips to make it more secure.
  3. Can I check multiple passwords at once?
    Currently, you can check one password at a time, but you can always paste in a new one after checking the first.
