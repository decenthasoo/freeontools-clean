FAQs About the Inflation Calculator

What is inflation?

Inflation is the rate at which the general level of prices for goods and services rises, and subsequently, the purchasing power of currency falls. This means that the same amount of money buys less over time.

Yep! You can compare prices from any year in history to the current year (or even a future year).
