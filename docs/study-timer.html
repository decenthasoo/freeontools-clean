What is a Study Timer?

Trying to stay on top of your studies without getting distracted? A Study Timer might be the secret sauce you need. If you’re a procrastinator or someone who easily gets lost in distractions, the Study Timer can be a total game-changer. Think of it like a workout for your brain—study hard, then take a breather. Rinse and repeat until you crush that study session!

How to Use the Study Timer

Getting started with the Study Timer is super simple. Here’s how it works:

  1. Open the Study Timer – Load up the tool in your browser or app.
  2. Set your study time – Choose how long you want to study in one session. Common intervals are 25-30 minutes, but you can adjust based on your needs.
  3. Set your break time – Typically, short breaks of 5-10 minutes work best, but again, it’s all customizable.
  4. Hit start – Start studying, and the timer will keep track of when to switch to your break.
  5. Enjoy your break – When the timer rings, stop and take a break. When the study timer starts again, get back to work!

Pro tip: Try using the Pomodoro Technique—study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After four sessions, take a longer 15-20 minute break. It helps you stay focused and not burn out.
