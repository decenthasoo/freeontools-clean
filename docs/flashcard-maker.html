What is a Flashcard Maker?

So, you need a way to study or review information quickly, right? That’s where a Flashcard Maker comes in. How to Use the Flashcard Maker

Building flashcards is a piece of cake. Here’s how to get started:

  1. Go to the Flashcard Maker tool – Open it up in your browser—no need to install anything.
  2. Create a deck – Start by creating a new deck of flashcards. You can give it a title, like “Spanish Vocabulary” or “Physics Formulas.”
  3. Add flashcards – For each card, input a question or concept on the front and the answer or explanation on the back. You can even add images or audio if that helps.
  4. Review and test – Once your flashcards are ready, go into review mode to test yourself. You’ll be able to see how well you know the material and which cards you need to revisit.
  5. Export or share – When you're done, export your deck to a flashcard app or share it with others. You can also print them if you like the old-school feel of paper cards.
