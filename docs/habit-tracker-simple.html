What is a Habit Tracker?

A Habit Tracker is a tool designed to help you monitor your daily habits, whether you're aiming to build new ones, like drinking more water or exercising regularly, or break old ones, like reducing late-night screen time. It acts as a personal accountability partner, tracking your progress without judgment, so you can stay focused on your goals.
