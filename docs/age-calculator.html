FAQs About the Age Calculator

How does the Age Calculator calculate age?

The Age Calculator uses the birthdate you input and subtracts it from the current date to calculate the exact age in years, months, and days.

Can I calculate age for a future birthdate?

This tool is designed to calculate the age as of today, so you can't calculate age for a future date. But you can easily check how old someone will be on a future birthday by plugging in that future date!

Does the Age Calculator account for leap years?

Yes, it does! The calculator takes leap years into account, so your results are always accurate—even if the birthdate falls on a leap year.

Can I use this tool to calculate the age of someone who passed away?

Absolutely. You can calculate the age of anyone, whether they are alive or have passed, by entering their birthdate and the date of their passing (or today’s date for a living person).
