FAQs About the Broken Link Checker

Will this Broken Link Checker work on any site?

Pretty much, yeah. As long as your site is live and public, you can scan it. Doesn’t matter if it’s WordPress, Shopify, Squarespace, or something else.

Does it check internal and external links?

Yup. The Broken Link Checker checks both internal links (within your own site) and external ones (that point to other sites). If either is broken, it’ll show up in your report.

How often should I run a Broken Link Checker?

At least once a month, especially if you update your content regularly or run a blog. Stuff changes fast—links break, pages move, entire websites vanish. Staying on top of it keeps things clean.

What kind of errors does it detect?

It flags common link errors like 404 Not Found, 403 Forbidden, 500 Server Errors, and timeout issues. It’s not just “this is broken”—it tells you why.

Do I need to install anything?

Nope. This Broken Link Checker is 100% browser-based. No downloads, no plugins, no bloat. Just you, your URL, and the truth.
