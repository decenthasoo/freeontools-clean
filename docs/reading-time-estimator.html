What is a Reading Time Estimator?

Ever opened a huge article or a long blog post and thought, "Am I really going to read all of this?" We've all been there. A Reading Time Estimator is a super simple tool that helps you figure out how long it’ll take you to read something based on the word count. It’s like the calculator of reading. You just pop in your text (or the link to it), and the tool will tell you how many minutes it’ll take to finish. It’s great for planning, timing, or just satisfying your curiosity about how much time you’re about to spend with that massive wall of text.

The best part? It’s a no-brainer. You don’t have to guess. The tool calculates your reading time based on average reading speeds. No math required (phew, right?).
