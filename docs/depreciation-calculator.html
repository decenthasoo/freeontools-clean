FAQs About the Depreciation Calculator

What is depreciation?

Depreciation is the reduction in value of an asset over time due to factors like wear and tear, aging, or obsolescence. It helps reflect the asset’s actual value compared to what it cost initially.

What’s the difference between straight-line and declining balance depreciation?

Straight-line: Depreciation is evenly spread out over the asset’s useful life.

Declining balance: Depreciation is higher in the earlier years and decreases over time.

Can I use the Depreciation Calculator for any type of asset?

Yes! Whether it’s a car, machinery, or a building, this tool works for any asset that depreciates over time.

Does the Depreciation Calculator account for repairs or maintenance?

No, this calculator only tracks depreciation. If you want to factor in maintenance or repair costs, you'll need to do that separately.
