FAQs About the Twitter Card Generator

What are Twitter Cards, exactly?

Twitter Cards are little media-rich previews that show up when someone tweets a link to your content. They include a title, image, description, and sometimes a video or app link—depending on the card type.

Do I need a Twitter Card Generator if I use WordPress?

Maybe not. Some plugins like Yoast handle cards automatically. But if you want full control—or you’re not using WordPress—this generator is the easiest way to go.

Will Twitter Cards work on every tweet?

They’ll show up when someone tweets a link to a page that has the right meta tags. That’s why using the Twitter Card Generator matters—it gives you those tags.

Can I use any image in my card?

Technically, yes—but keep it clean and make sure it follows Twitter’s guidelines. Recommended size is 1200x628 pixels for summary with image cards.

What happens if I don’t add card tags?

Twitter might just show the link without a preview, or it might guess what to show. Spoiler alert: Twitter is not great at guessing.
