What is a Passive Voice Detector?

Alright, let’s talk about Passive Voice Detector. It’s that little tool that’ll help you figure out if your sentences are getting a little too lazy. We’ve all been there, right? You’re writing something, and your sentences start to feel… off. Not bad, just passive. The Passive Voice Detector catches that for you.

For example, instead of saying “The ball was thrown by Tom,” the active voice version would be “Tom threw the ball.” See the difference? In the first version, the focus is on the action happening to the ball. In the second one, Tom’s the star. A Passive Voice Detector helps you spot when you’ve fallen into the passive trap, making your writing clearer, stronger, and more engaging.
