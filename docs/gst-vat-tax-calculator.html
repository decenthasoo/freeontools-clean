What is a GST/VAT Tax Calculator?

Alright, if you’ve ever bought something or sold something, you’ve probably noticed that extra bit added to the price—the GST (Goods and Services Tax) or VAT (Value Added Tax). Now, figuring out how much tax you’re paying (or charging) can be a headache without the right tool. That’s where the GST/VAT Tax Calculator comes in. It does all the math for you! Whether you’re adding tax to a price or working out how much tax was included in a price, this tool helps you get the numbers right every time.
