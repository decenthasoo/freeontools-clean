How to Use a Daily Goal Setter

  1. Go to the tool page: Open the Daily Goal Setter tool. It’s available on any device, so whether you’re on your phone or computer, you’re good to go.
  2. Add your goals: Start typing in the goals you want to achieve today. They don’t have to be huge—just whatever you want to focus on. Maybe it’s answering emails, finishing a report, or finally organizing your closet.
  3. Assign times (optional): If you like to get super specific, you can set a time for each goal. This keeps you focused and helps you stay on track. Or, you can just leave it open-ended.
  4. Hit “Add Goal”: After you’ve got your goals listed, hit the “Add Goal” button. You’ll get a clear breakdown of what your day looks like.
  5. Get to work!: Now that you’ve got your goals set, it’s time to crush them. As you complete each one, mark it off. That little checkmark feels so satisfying.

Pro tip : If you’re feeling extra productive, you can add a bonus goal or two. That’ll give you something to aim for if you finish your regular goals early!
