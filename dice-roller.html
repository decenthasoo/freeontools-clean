What Is a Dice Roller?

Okay, simple version? A Dice Roller is a digital tool that mimics rolling dice. You tell it how many dice you want, what type (D6, D20, D10—yeah, we’re getting nerdy), and boom. It spits out some numbers, just like tossing physical dice… minus the part where they roll under your couch.

You can use it for board games, D&D, drinking games, school stuff, or even just to make boring decisions way more exciting.
