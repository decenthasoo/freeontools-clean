What is a SERP Preview Tool?

So, you're working on your website and you’ve got your SEO all set—meta tags, optimized content, and snappy titles. But wait, how does your site actually look in Google’s search results? This is where the SERP Preview Tool comes in. It’s a tool that lets you see a preview of how your page will look in the search engine results pages (SERPs). Essentially, it helps you understand how your title, description, and other elements will appear when someone Googles your site.

Why should you care? Well, the way your page looks in search results can impact whether people click on it or scroll right past. If your title and description are too long or don’t match what users are searching for, you might lose potential visitors. A SERP preview shows you how to tweak things so your results pop and attract more clicks.
